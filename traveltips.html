Make Your Journey Smoother – Practical Tips for Smart Travel

After nearly two decades of exploring both India and the world — from serene South Indian backwaters to road-tripping across Tasmania — I've picked up countless small tricks that make a big difference while traveling.

This section brings together everything I’ve learned: packing hacks, cultural cues, apps that help, and things I wish I knew earlier. Whether you're planning your first trip or your fiftieth, I hope these tips help make your travels more comfortable, safe, and memorable.
